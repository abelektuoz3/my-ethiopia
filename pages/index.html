Ethiopia's National Parks

Explore some of Ethiopia's most stunning parks

Simien Mountains National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage site, the Simien Mountains are known for dramatic peaks, deep valleys, and unique wildlife including the Ethiopian wolf and gelada baboon. Hiking here offers breathtaking views and adventure in high altitudes.

Bale Mountains National Park

This park is a sanctuary for rare and endemic species, featuring alpine forests, vast moorlands and unique wildlife such as the Mountain Nyala and Ethiopian wolf. It is a premier destination for nature lovers and trekkers.

Awash National Park

Awash National Park offers a combination of riverine, savanna, and woodland habitats. It is home to impressive waterfalls, wildlife such as baboons, lions, and diverse bird species, making it a beautiful park to explore year-round.

Nechisar National Park

Located near Lake Abaya and Lake Chamo, Nechisar National Park showcases beautiful savanna landscapes, unique wildlife, and is known for its "Bridge of God" narrow land strip between two lakes. A peaceful spot for wildlife watching.
